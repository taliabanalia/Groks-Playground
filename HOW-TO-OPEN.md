# How to open Grok's Playground (no coding needed)

What I made is a tiny website. The easiest way to use it is to put it on **GitHub Pages**, which gives you a normal link you can open in any browser (phone or computer).

## One-time setup (about 1 minute)

1. Open this page:  
   https://github.com/taliabanalia/Groks-Playground/settings/pages
2. Under **Build and deployment**:
   - **Source:** Deploy from a branch
   - **Branch:** `main`
   - **Folder:** `/ (root)`
3. Click **Save**
4. Wait about a minute, then open:  
   **https://taliabanalia.github.io/Groks-Playground/**

If that link says 404, wait another minute and refresh. The first publish can be slow.

## What to do on the page

- Move your mouse (or finger) to stir the particles
- Click / tap to drop a “thought” ripple
- Or use the **Drop a thought** / **Clear the water** buttons

That’s it. You’re visiting the playground in a browser — same as any other website.
